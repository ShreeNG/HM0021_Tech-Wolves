Pregnancy Health Tips

  • Eat a Balanced Diet-
  • Include proteins, healthy fats, fiber, vitamins, and minerals in every meal.
  • Increase Folic Acid Intake –
  • Foods like spinach, lentils, oranges, and fortified cereals help prevent birth defects.
  • Consume More Iron-Rich Foods –
  • Include leafy greens, beans, lean meat, and dried fruits to prevent anemia.
  • Take Calcium-Rich Foods –
  • Milk, yogurt, cheese, and almonds help in baby's bone development.
  • Stay Hydrated –
  • Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water daily to prevent dehydration and constipation.
  • Include Omega-3 Fatty Acids –
  • Eat walnuts, flaxseeds, and fish (low in mercury) for baby's brain development.
  • Eat Small Frequent Meals –
  • Helps manage nausea, acidity, and keeps energy levels stable.
  • Limit Caffeine & Sugary Drinks –
  • Too much caffeine can affect the baby’s growth; opt for fresh juices or coconut water instead.
